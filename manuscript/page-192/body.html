Tie; Tido; Deo; Ligo. These in their primary sense signify the property or attraction of the firmament.

Tongue; Tafod; Glossa; Lingua, the flow of things.

Top; Crib; Kolophon; Fastigium, the sky or covering the high end.

Touch; Cnithio; Thego, Psallo; Tango, acting upon the upper covering or skin.

Track; Ol; Ichnos; Vestigium, the shadow or sign of a person or other thing going before another, or a continued action upon the ground.

Tread; Troedio; Pateo; Calco, to put the foot upon the ground or pat.

Trifle; Overbeth; Leros; Nugamentum, possession without place, or a thing without spring.

Triumph; Gorfoledd; Thriambos; Triumphus, the praise of a man.
